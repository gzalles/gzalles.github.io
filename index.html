About Me

I am a 23 year old graduate student at NYU Steinhardt from La Paz, Bolivia. I am currently doing my masters in Music Technology with a specialization in spatial audio research. When I am not learning how to become a better programmer and artist I like to play electric guitar, spend time with my family or learn a new skill - whatever it may be. My other interests involve recording and producing records, tinkering with electronics, and playing video games. I really like collaborating with people and learning from them - I think that everyone has something to offer and that the best results occur when people work together.
