About Me

I am graduate student at NYU. I am from Bolivia. I am 23 years old. I love playing guitar, spending time with my family and working with technology. My interests involve recording and producing records, tinkering with electronics, coding, and generally making cool stuff. Over the last few months I have been getting more involved with VR by learning how to use and implement ambisonic technology with VR content and even started prototyping my own microphone! In the future I am thinking of doing a PhD in a field related to technology and education or maybe working for a startup that is using technology. I like collaborating with people and learning from then, I think that everyone has something to offer.
