about

Hello, my name is Gabriel Zalles. I am a Bolivian student living in San Diego and attending UCSD where I am working towards a PhD in Computer Music. I am an audio engineer specializing in the field of immersive audio, specifically ambisonics. I have experience developing software with MATLAB, JUCE (C++), and also do web development in my spare time. I additionally thoroughly enjoy designing hardware. For my masters thesis at NYU I designed a FOA microphone with MEMS capsules. My design explored the effect of capsule coincidence a feature afforded by the form factor of these capsules. I love learning about 3D printing, laser cutting, PCB design and general hardware manufacturing processes. When I'm not working I like to play tennis and make music with my friends.
