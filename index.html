About Me

I am graduate student at NYU. I am from Bolivia. I am 23 years old. I love playing guitar, spending time with my family and working with technology. My interests involve recording, producing, tinkering, coding and making cool stuff, especially if that stuff can inspire people or directly help them. Over the last few semesters I have been getting more involved with VR by learning how to use and implement ambisonic technology for VR content and even started prototyping my own microphone! In the future I am thinking of doing a PhD in a field related to technology and education or maybe working for a startup that is doing social work using technology. I like collaborating with people and learning from then, I think that everyone has something to offer.
