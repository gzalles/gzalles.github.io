Origenes

Origenes means origins in spanish. The title for this EP derived from a realization that I was losing my identity as a Bolivian citizen. I have been living in the United States for a long time and I want to assimilate very badly - fitting in has always been hard. This project was a reminder that there is a life I left behind. I wrote this EP as my final project at UCSD. All the songs in this EP are in spanish. I have a few others songs on my soundcloud page.

This project was als an attempt to fuse spanish rock with traditional Bolivian instruments. In the process of making these songs I implemented some Kontakt libraries with pan flutes and charangos. I made all the songs alone, from beginning to end using Ableton. I think the hardest part was getting out of my shell as a singer and trying to be objective when mixing and mastering. The final presentation consisted of me singing these songs on an acoustic guitar in front of faculty and friends at UCSD.
